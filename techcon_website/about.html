Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of engineers and entrepreneurs passionate about technology. Over the years, it has grown into an internationally recognized event, drawing thousands of attendees from across the globe to explore the latest advancements in AI, robotics, software engineering, and more.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, empower creators, and connect global leaders in technology. We believe that by fostering collaboration and sharing knowledge, we can shape a better future for society through technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Alice Johnson

A pioneer in artificial intelligence, Dr. Johnson has contributed groundbreaking research in natural language processing. Her keynote at TechCon 2018 inspired a new wave of AI-driven startups.

Mr. David Kim

As the CEO of InnovateX, David Kim has led transformative projects in renewable energy technology. His talk in 2020 emphasized sustainability through innovation.

Ms. Sofia Martínez

A leading voice in cybersecurity, Sofia Martínez has worked with international organizations to promote digital safety. Her workshop at TechCon 2022 highlighted strategies for protecting user data.
